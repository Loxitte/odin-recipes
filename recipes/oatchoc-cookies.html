Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

These filling and flavorful cookies were a staple in my house growing up. Nothing satisfies a sweet tooth quite like them. You can even convince yourself they're a breakfast food if you're imaginitive enough!

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour: 1 cup
  • Baking soda: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Salt: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Unsalted softened butter: 1/2 cup
  • Granulated sugar: 1/3 cup
  • Light brown sugar: 2/3 cup
  • Pure vanilla extract: 2 teaspoons
  • Large egg: 1
  • Rolled oats: 1 1/2 cup
  • Semisweet chocolate chips: 1 cup

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour and baking soda then set aside.
  2. Combine the butter with both sugars in a large bowl. Beat until light and fluffy.
  3. Add the salt, vanilla, and eggs to the sugar mixture. Beat until well mixed.
  4. Add flour mixture. Mix until almost combined.
  5. Stir in the oats and chocolate chips.
  6. If you have the time and patience, store and chill dough in the fridge for one hour to overnight.
  7. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 C).
  8. Line 2 baking sheets or pans with parchment paper.
  9. Use a small ice cream scooper or large spoon to drop heaping tablespoon-sized balls of dough onto the parchment-lined pans.
  10. Bake about 8 to 10 minutes, or until cookies are golden aroung the edges but still soft in the center.
  11. Remove from oven and let cool on baking sheets for 1-2 minutes. Transfer to a wire rack and let cool completely.
  12. Enjoy alone or with milk or cold drink of choice!
