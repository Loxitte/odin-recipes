Blueberry Muffins

Description

A beautiful mix of sweet muffins and tart blueberries. This one is a favorite of Austin's, and he has the secret to making them extra moist and delicious!

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour: 1 1/2 cups
  • Granulated sugar: 3/4 cup
  • Baking powder: 2 teaspoons
  • Fine salt: 1/4 teaspoon
  • Vegetable oil: 1/3 cup
  • Large egg: 1
  • Milk of choice: 1/3 cup
  • Vanilla extract: 1 1/2 teaspoons
  • Fresh or frozen blueberries: 1 cup

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (204 degrees C).
  2. Line muffin cups with paper liners (8 for big muffins, 10 for standard size).
  3. Lightly grease or spray top of pan with oil of choice to prevent muffin tops from sticking to pan.
  4. In a large bowl, whick together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  5. In a small bowl or measuring jug that holds at least 1 cup, add oil and crack in the egg. Pour enough milk to reach the 1-cup line of the jug. Add vanilla extract and whisk until combined.
  6. Add wet ingredients to the dry ingredients. Stire gently until combined.
  7. Gently fold in the blueberries. Do not overmix! The batter will be thick.
  8. Divide batter between the muffin cups. If you want big-topped muffins, fill to the top of the cups.
  9. Sprinkle a little sugar on top of each muffin.
  10. Bake muffins for 15 to 20 minutes or until the tops are no longer wet and the toothpick inserted in the center comes out with crumbs, not wet batter.
  11. Let cool for a few minutes before transferring baked muffins to a drying rack.
